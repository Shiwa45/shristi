Custom Bi-Fold Brochures (A Classic Format for a Professional Message)

The classic bi-fold brochure offers a clean, organized four-panel layout, perfect for presenting your brand with clarity and impact. Our custom printing service elevates this format, using premium, thick paper stocks and a flawless folding process. The result is a Sturdy booklet that feels professional in your customer's hands and gives your message the quality it deserves.

  • Choose Your Premium Paper: Select our smooth 300 GSM Standard Artboard for vibrant prints, or the elegant 300 GSM Ivory Texture Board for a sophisticated, tactile feel that signals luxury.
  • A Flawless, Crack-Resistant Fold: We professionally score (crease) every brochure before folding. This crucial step ensures a sharp, clean fold line that prevents the thick paper and ink from cracking, maintaining a pristine finish.
  • The Ideal Format for Detailed Content: The four-panel layout is perfect for creating professional restaurant menus, detailed real estate listings, informative event programs, and impressive corporate profiles.
  • Print on One or Both Sides: Choose to print on the outside panels for a simple leaflet, or select double-sided printing to utilize all four panels for a complete, in-depth presentation.
  • Fast 3-Day Dispatch: Your custom folded brochures are printed, proofed, and shipped quickly via our trusted courier partners after you approve the final design proof.

Top Questions Answered Here

If we missed anything, we are just a call away.

What's the difference between a bi-fold and a tri-fold brochure?

A bi-fold (or half-fold) is a single sheet folded in half, creating four panels (front, back, and two inside). A tri-fold is a sheet folded into thirds, creating six smaller panels. Bi-folds are excellent for larger images and a more open layout.

How should I arrange content in a four-panel layout?

Think of it like a booklet. The front panel is your cover. The two inside panels work together as a spread for your main content. The back panel is for contact information, a map, or a closing message.

What are my 300 GSM paper options?

Choose Standard Artboard for a classic, smooth surface and vibrant print. Opt for Ivory Texture Board for an elegant, tactile feel perfect for luxury brands. Our Matte Finish Board provides a modern, non-reflective surface.

Will the brochure crack along the fold?

No. We score (crease) every brochure before folding it. This essential step creates a clean guide that prevents the thick 300 GSM paper and ink from cracking, ensuring a sharp, professional fold every time.

What sizes are available for bi-fold brochures?

Our standard bi-fold is created from a flat A4 sheet, which folds into a convenient A5-sized booklet. However, we can accommodate a wide range of custom sizes to fit your specific design.

Can I print on both sides?

Yes, absolutely. You can choose single-sided printing for a simple card-style leaflet, or double-sided printing to utilize all four panels for a complete presentation.

Will I see a proof before my brochures are printed?

Yes, always. We will send a free digital proof of your flat design showing the fold line for your final approval. Printing only begins after you are completely satisfied.

What file formats do you accept for printing?

We accept high-resolution PDF, AI, and PSD files. For the best results, we highly recommend providing your artwork as a press-quality PDF with a 3mm bleed.

What is the minimum quantity I can order?

The MOQ is 100 brochures, making it accessible for small businesses and event organizers to create professional marketing materials.

How quickly will I receive my order?

Your order will be printed and dispatched within 3 business days after you have approved the digital proof.
