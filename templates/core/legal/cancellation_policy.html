Cancellation Policy

Important information about order cancellations

At Shrishti Printing, we understand that circumstances may change. Please review our cancellation policy to understand your options and any applicable fees.

General Cancellation Terms

Cancellation fees depend on the stage of processing your order has reached at the time of cancellation request. The earlier you cancel, the lower the fee.

Free Cancellation Window

Free Cancellation within 30 Minutes

Customers can cancel their order free of charge within 30 minutes of placing the booking. After this window, cancellation fees will apply based on the processing stage.

Cancellation Fee Structure

Cancellation fees are calculated based on how far your order has progressed:

Stage 1: Order Received (30 min - 2 hours)

Cancellation Fee: 10% of order value

Order has been received but processing has not begun.

Stage 2: Design/Proof Phase (2-24 hours)

Cancellation Fee: 25% of order value

Artwork is being prepared or proof has been sent for approval.

Stage 3: Pre-Production (After proof approval)

Cancellation Fee: 50% of order value

Proof has been approved and materials are being prepared for printing.

Stage 4: In Production

Cancellation Fee: 100% of order value (No Refund)

Printing has started or completed. Order cannot be cancelled.

Stage 5: Completed/Dispatched

No Cancellation Available

Order has been completed and shipped. Cancellation not possible.

Cancellation Due to Non-Payment

Shrishti Printing reserves the right to cancel orders in the following circumstances:

  • Payment is not received within the specified time frame
  • Payment method fails or is declined
  • Dishonoured cheques (subject to additional fees of Rs. 1,000 plus bank charges)
  • Customer fails to respond to communication regarding order details or proof approval within 7 days

In such cases, any advance payment made will be refunded minus a 10% processing fee.

How to Cancel Your Order

To request a cancellation:

  1. Contact Us Immediately: Email us at info@shrishtiprinting.com or call our customer service.
  2. Provide Order Details: Include your order number and reason for cancellation.
  3. Confirmation: We will confirm the cancellation and applicable fees within 24 hours.
  4. Refund Processing: If applicable, refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days.

Custom/Personalized Orders

Important: Custom or personalized orders cannot be cancelled once production begins, as these items are made specifically for you and cannot be resold.

Order Modification

If you need to make changes to your order rather than cancel it completely, please contact us as soon as possible. Minor modifications may be possible depending on the stage of production, though additional fees may apply.

Force Majeure

Shrishti Printing reserves the right to cancel or delay orders due to circumstances beyond our control, including but not limited to natural disasters, strikes, equipment failure, or material shortages. In such cases, customers will receive a full refund of any payments made.
