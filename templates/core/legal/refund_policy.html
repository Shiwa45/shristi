Refund Policy

Understanding our refund terms

At Shrishti Printing, we strive to deliver high-quality printing services that meet your expectations. Please review our refund policy carefully before placing an order.

General Refund Policy

  • Payment Required in Advance: All orders require payment in advance before production begins.
  • No Refunds After Production: Once an order has entered the production phase, payment will not be refunded.
  • Customer Responsibility: Shrishti Printing is not liable for errors in proof or artwork that were approved by the customer.

Proof Approval and Customer Responsibility

Before production begins, we provide a digital proof for your review and approval. By approving the proof, you accept full responsibility for:

  • Spelling, grammar, and content accuracy
  • Image quality and placement
  • Colors (subject to printing limitations)
  • Layout and design elements

No refunds will be issued for errors that were present in the approved proof.

Eligible Refund Cases

Refunds or reprints may be considered in the following circumstances:

  • Manufacturing Defects: If the printed product has defects due to our production error (not present in the approved proof).
  • Incorrect Specifications: If we failed to follow the specifications confirmed in your order (e.g., wrong paper type, incorrect quantity).
  • Damaged Delivery: If products are damaged during shipping due to insufficient packaging on our part (must be reported within 48 hours of delivery with photographic evidence).
  • Non-Delivery: If we fail to deliver your order and cannot provide a suitable alternative delivery date.

Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will NOT be provided in the following situations:

  • Customer-supplied artwork contains errors that were not caught during proof approval
  • Customer changes mind after production has started
  • Minor color variations between digital proof and printed product (due to screen calibration differences and printing process limitations)
  • Standard industry tolerances for cutting, trimming, or folding (typically +/- 1/8 inch)
  • Customer dissatisfaction with design choices they approved
  • Late payment penalties or interest charges

Refund Request Process

If you believe you are eligible for a refund:

  1. Contact Us Immediately: Email us at info@shrishtiprinting.com or call us within 48 hours of receiving your order.
  2. Provide Details: Include your order number, description of the issue, and photographic evidence of the defect or error.
  3. Review Process: Our team will review your claim within 2-3 business days.
  4. Resolution: If approved, we will offer either a full refund, partial refund, or reprint of the order at no additional cost.

Refund Timeline

If a refund is approved, it will be processed within 7-10 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

Late Payment

Interest at the rate of 15% per month will be charged if the bill is not paid within the due date. Late payment penalties are non-refundable.
