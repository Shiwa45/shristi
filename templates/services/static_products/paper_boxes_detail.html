Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our folding carton boxes

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity is 100 pieces for folding carton boxes. Larger quantities offer better pricing due to press and finishing efficiencies.

Do you provide dieline templates?

Yes, we provide free dieline templates for all box styles. Share your size and style requirements, and we'll send you a print-ready dieline for artwork placement.

What file formats do you accept?

We require artwork in high-resolution PDF format with 0.125 inches (3mm) bleed. For best results, use CMYK color mode and 300 DPI resolution.

How long does production take?

Standard production time is 7-10 business days after artwork approval. Rush orders can be accommodated with additional charges.

Can I get samples before placing a large order?

Absolutely! We offer sample packs for ₹500 that include different paper types and finishes. The sample cost is deducted from your final order.
