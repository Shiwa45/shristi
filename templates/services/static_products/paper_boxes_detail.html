What file formats do you accept?

We accept PDF and AI files (300 DPI, CMYK, 3 mm bleed). Please include cut marks/die lines on a separate layer.

What is the minimum order quantity?

{% if product.minimum_quantity %}Minimum {{ product.minimum_quantity }} pcs for {{ product.name }}.{% else %}Minimum quantity depends on your chosen specification — see the quantity selector above.{% endif %} Bulk discounts apply automatically.

Can you make custom box sizes?

Yes — contact us with your exact dimensions (L × W × H in mm) and we'll quote a custom die for your box.

Will I get a proof before production?

Yes. We always share a digital proof and a die-line preview via WhatsApp or email before going to press.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are manufactured and dispatched within 3–5 business days after proof approval. Metro cities: 1–2 extra days; tier 2/3 cities: 3–5 extra days.

What payment methods are accepted?

UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking and wallets. All transactions are 100% secure and include a GST invoice.
